The Cultural Committee, IIM Kozhikode

At IIM Kozhikode, one of India’s most prestigious management institutes and ranked NIRF #3, the Cultural Committee serves as the epicenter of student life, bringing vibrancy and unity to our campus through diverse cultural events throughout the year!

With 10 years of experience under our belt, we have conducted 100+ events that create memorable experiences to bring students together and promote holistic growth
